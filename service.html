Education and Outreach Programs

Provide information about cancer and ways to reduce the risk of cancer through outreach and education programs. This could include information on lifestyle choices, such as healthy eating, exercise, and avoiding tobacco and alcohol.

Cancer Screening Services

Provide cancer screening services to help detect cancer early, when it may be more easily treatable. This could include mammograms for breast cancer, colonoscopies for colon cancer, and other types of screening tests.

Support Groups

Offer support groups for cancer patients, survivors, and their families. These groups can provide emotional support and resources for coping with cancer and its impact.

Patient Navigation Services

Provide patient navigation services to help guide cancer patients and their families through the healthcare system. This could include help with scheduling appointments, navigating insurance coverage, and accessing resources and support services.

Fundraising and Advocacy

Raise funds for cancer research and advocacy for policies that support cancer patients and their families.

Survivorship Programs

Offer programs that focus on the needs of cancer survivors, including physical and emotional health, financial support, and community building.

Counseling and Mental Health Services

Provide counseling and mental health services for cancer patients, survivors, and their families to address the psychological and emotional impact of cancer.
