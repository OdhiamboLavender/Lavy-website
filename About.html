Social Responsibility

We are committed to ;

  1. Reducing stigma: The website can work to reduce the stigma around mental health issues by providing information on how to talk about mental health, stories of people who have struggled with mental health, and resources for breaking down stigma.
  2. Promoting self-care: The website can provide tips on how to practice self-care, including techniques for managing stress, relaxation techniques, and self-care activities.
  3. Offering support: The website can offer support to students who are struggling with mental health issues. This can include access to online support groups, forums, or chat rooms.
