Section 2 CANCER AWARENESS

From the recent past, the entire health sector in the world has been in a very critical condition due to the cancer pandemic. An issue that has left many people speechless. Cancer is dangerous and refers to the diseases that happen as a result of abnormal growth and division of cells. There are several types of cancers, the most common types affecting men are lung, throat, stomach, colon and blood cancer, while those commonly affecting women include breast, lung, stomach, colon and cervical cancer.

Section 3 OBJECTIVES

  1. Create awareness and remove the stigma and fear attached.
  2. Help people recognize the early signs and symptoms of cancer thus enabling them to seek treatment at an early stage.
  3. Educate people about the key risk factors, since more than 30% of cancer cases could be prevented by modifying lifestyle or avoiding the risk factors.
  4. Inform people about the importance of regular screening and checkups.
